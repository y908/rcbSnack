Welcome to RATVM!

We are a student-run RCB project with one driving mission: to provide the snacks that YOU want, cheaply and conveniently. Let us know what you're craving! We will do our best to provide what the student body desires, because we know that a hungry coder is a distracted coder, and we want to contribute to an atmosphere of academic success!

How it Works

Snacks will be located in the general area of the Atrium.
Anyone is welcome to buy snacks.
We are using an honor system for payment. Please place your bills or change in the box provided.
This is a non-profit project. All of the money collected is used to buy more snacks.
If there is a snack you'd like to have, please make a request.
